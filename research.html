Learn from science giants
After my PhD graduation in 2014, I have been working with Dr. Y.M. Dennis Lo and Dr. Rossa W.K. Chiu, both of which are my mentors and science giants. Dr. Lo is known as the "father of noninvasive prenatal testing" and a pioneer in cancer liquid biopsy. He is the recipient of numerous awards, including the King Faisal International Prize in Medicine in 2014 and the Future Science Prize - Life Science Prize in 2016. He is a Fellow of the Royal Society in 2011 and Foreign Associate of the US National Academy of Sciences in 2013. Dr. Chiu is a Fellow of the Royal College of Pathologists of Australasia and has been awarded 2011 International Federation of Clinical Chemistry and Laboratory Medicine (IFCC) Young Investigator Award, the 2012 China Women in Science Fellowship. I am lucy to get trained by these world-class scientists for critical thinking as well as "hypothesize boldly, while prove carefully".
